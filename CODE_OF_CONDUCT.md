# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in EBICSMIT free from harassment for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity and
orientation, or technology choices.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, protect confidential information,
and keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and project safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, deliberate disclosure of private
information, and publishing bank credentials or customer financial data are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code.
Good-faith security reports must use the private path in `SECURITY.md`.

This text is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
